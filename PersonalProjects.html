Adobe Photoshop

These Adobe Photoshop projects are from my Fundamentals of Digital Media class. Each project displays different skills that I learned in Photoshop.

Project Green

This piece uses the cropping and brush tools. The aim of this project was to take several images at the greenhouse located at UC and to make a postcard for an imaginary UC Plant Club.

Verticality

Verticality was a project I did that involved changing the color and opacity of layers to create the illusion that the images were part of the walls.

Panels

In Panels I had to take a picture of someone I knew, find a piece of art, and find a quote from a poet. This piece uses a gradient for the background and practices the typography tool.

Fantastical Night at UC

Fantastical Night at UC was the final project for this class. I based this piece off of the DLC for Destiny 2: Foresaken.

In this DLC, a character named Cayde-6 is murdered. I chose to embody the five stages of grief in this project: Denial, Bargaining, Anger, Depression, and Acceptance.

Research Papers

My senior year of high school, I wrote a twelve page paper that discussed whether or not Artificial Intelligence is a threat to humanity.
I state both sides of the argument and then follow up with my viewpoint. I think that Artificial Intelligence and Humanity work better hand-in-hand.

I wrote a paper for my Implications of IT class that discusses the effects video games have on society.
